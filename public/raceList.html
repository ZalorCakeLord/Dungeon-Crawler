Race Descriptions (Only for flavor rn.)

  • Human: Humans are typically of average height with diverse skin tones and hair colors. They are known for their adaptability and resilience.
  • Elf: Elves are tall and slender, often with pointed ears and ethereal features. Their skin may range from pale to deep shades, and their hair is usually long and flowing.
  • Dwarf: Dwarves are stocky and muscular, with broad shoulders and short stature. They often have thick beards and rugged features, reflecting their sturdy nature.
  • Orc: Orcs are large and imposing, with green or gray skin, prominent tusks, and a fierce demeanor. Their muscular build is a testament to their warrior culture.
  • Halfling: Halflings are small and nimble, usually no taller than three feet. They have round faces and curly hair, giving them a friendly and approachable appearance.
  • Gnome: Gnomes are petite with whimsical features, often sporting bright hair and clever expressions. Their curious nature is reflected in their lively eyes.
  • Tiefling: Tieflings have distinct features, including horns, pointed tails, and reddish or purplish skin. Their eyes often glow with an otherworldly light.
  • Dragonborn: Dragonborn are tall and powerful, resembling humanoid dragons with scales in various colors. Their draconic heritage gives them a commanding presence.
  • Aasimar: Aasimars possess celestial features, often with radiant skin and bright, almost glowing eyes. They may have a halo-like glow that sets them apart.
  • Half-Orc: Half-Orcs are robust and strong, with a mix of human and orc features. They often have prominent tusks and a fierce countenance.
  • Dark Elf: Dark Elves have ebony skin and white or silver hair, with striking red or purple eyes. Their ethereal beauty is often shadowy and mysterious.
  • Goblin: Goblins are small and wiry, with greenish skin and large ears. Their mischievous grins and sharp features give them a crafty appearance.
  • Lizardfolk: Lizardfolk have reptilian features, including scaled skin, elongated snouts, and sharp claws. Their cold-blooded nature gives them a calculating demeanor.
  • Tabaxi: Tabaxi are feline humanoids with lithe bodies, covered in fur with whiskers and sharp claws. Their eyes are often bright and expressive.
  • Warforged: Warforged are constructs made of metal and magic, with a humanoid shape. They possess an impressive and durable appearance, often showing signs of wear from battle.
  • Half-Elf: Half-Elves combine the grace of elves and the versatility of humans, often possessing slightly pointed ears and a unique blend of features.
  • Vampire: Vampires often have pale skin and alluring features, with sharp fangs that hint at their predatory nature. Their eyes may glint with an unnatural allure.
  • Cyborg: Cyborgs blend organic and mechanical components, with visible cybernetic enhancements and a unique fusion of human and machine traits.
  • Android: Androids are humanoid robots, often with a sleek, metallic exterior. They possess an expressionless face and exhibit an aura of advanced technology.
  • Alien: Aliens may vary widely in appearance, often featuring unusual skin colors, shapes, and appendages that defy earthly norms.
  • Shapeshifter: Shapeshifters can alter their form, often appearing as a blend of various races or with an ambiguous physicality that makes them unpredictable.
  • Undead: Undead beings show signs of decay, with pale or rotting skin and lifeless eyes. Their movements may be jerky or unnatural, reflecting their unholy nature.
  • Demigod: Demigods often possess striking features that hint at their divine heritage, such as glowing skin or ethereal beauty, marking them as beings of great power.
  • Fairy: Fairies are tiny, winged beings with delicate features and vibrant colors. Their playful appearance is complemented by shimmering wings that sparkle in the light.
  • Troll: Trolls are massive and hulking, with tough, mottled skin and grotesque features. Their size and strength make them formidable foes.
  • Centaur: Centaurs have the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse, combining strength and speed in a unique, powerful form.
